<div style="text-align: justify;">
  <h3 style="color: #005ea5;">About the Group</h3>
  <p>
    Located in Shenzhen, the <strong>Eric Research Group</strong> at <a href="https://www.hitsz.edu.cn" target="_blank">Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen (HITSZ)</a> is a leading interdisciplinary research team specializing in <strong>2D materials</strong> and <strong>atomic-precision (Ångström-scale) manufacturing</strong>.
  </p>
  <p>
    Our mission is to bridge the gap between fundamental materials science and industrial semiconductor applications. We specialize in designing, fabricating, and characterizing novel 2D layered materials, leveraging cutting-edge additive manufacturing techniques to push the limits of semiconductor device miniaturization and performance.
  </p>
  <p>
    We are dedicated to developing next-generation electronic and optoelectronic devices that will power the future of artificial intelligence, quantum computing, and flexible electronics.
  </p>
