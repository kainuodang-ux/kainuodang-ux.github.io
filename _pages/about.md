# About the Eric Research Group

## Our Vision
To become a global leader in 2D material-based semiconductor technology, driving innovation from lab to fab for a smarter and more connected world.

## Our Story
Established at Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen (HITSZ), the Eric Research Group is at the forefront of the global race to develop post-silicon semiconductor technologies. As Moore’s Law approaches its physical limits, 2D materials have emerged as the most promising candidates to enable the next generation of ultra-thin, flexible, and high-performance electronic devices.

Our group uniquely combines expertise in **2D materials science** with cutting-edge **ångström-scale additive manufacturing**. This synergy allows us to not only discover new materials but also precisely engineer their atomic structures for targeted semiconductor applications—an approach that accelerates the translation of fundamental research into real-world technologies.

## Our Shenzhen Advantage
Based in Shenzhen, the "Silicon Valley of China," we benefit from the city's unique position as a global hub for advanced manufacturing and innovation. We enjoy strong collaborations with leading universities and research institutions worldwide, as well as close ties with the semiconductor industry in the Greater Bay Area (GBA), facilitating rapid technology transfer and commercialization.

## Research Philosophy
We believe in **curiosity-driven research with impact**. Our work is guided by three core principles:
1. **Atomic Precision**: Controlling matter at the ångström scale (10⁻¹⁰ meters) to unlock new material properties.
2. **Cross-Disciplinarity**: Breaking down barriers between materials science, engineering, and physics.
3. **Industrial Relevance**: Aligning our research with the needs of the global semiconductor industry.
